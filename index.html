Our Recruitment Process

Recruitment Process at Company

01

Client Consultation

Ramel Consultants engages with clients to understand their hiring needs, role specifications, and organisational culture to ensure precise candidate alignment.

02

Talent Sourcing

The firm utilises an extensive talent database, Ramels’ ATS, and industry networks to identify potential candidates who meet the specified qualifications and experience requirements.

03

Screening & Assessment

The firm conducts initial screening and assessment, including background checks and skill evaluations, to shortlist candidates with the highest alignment to client needs.

04

Candidate Presentation

Shortlisted candidates are presented to clients with detailed profiles and assessment reports, providing all necessary information for informed decision-making.

05

Interview Coordination

The firm manages the scheduling and coordination of interviews between the client and candidates, ensuring a streamlined interview process.

06

Offer and Onboarding

The firm assists with offer negotiations, candidate acceptance, and onboarding support to facilitate a smooth transition into the client’s organization.
